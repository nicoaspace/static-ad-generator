## BRAND OVERVIEW

**Name:** Siigo  
**Product:** Siigo Facturación Electrónica  
**Tagline:** "El software administrativo y contable 100% en la nube"

**Founders:** Ricardo Ortiz & Fernando Rebellon

**Voice Adjectives [5]:**
1. Cercano — warm and approachable, speaks like a trusted partner, not a corporate vendor
2. Empoderador — empowers entrepreneurs and accountants; "transforms lives"
3. Directo — clear, action-first language; heavy use of imperatives ("¡Empieza hoy!", "¡Comprar ahora!")

Siigo is the #1 cloud-based accounting and administrative software in Colombia, authorized by the DIAN for electronic invoicing. Targeting SMEs and independent accountants (contadores) across LATAM. Mission: "transformar la vida de nuestro equipo, contadores y empresarios."

**Competitive Differentiation:**
- vs. **Alegra** (main competitor): Siigo leads in accounting depth — more granular financial reports, specialized tax tools, broader ERP modules. Alegra bundles more features at lower tiers; Siigo wins on compliance depth and market authority (#1 DIAN-authorized).
- vs. **World Office**: Enterprise-focused, slow UX. Siigo wins on cloud-native speed, SME focus, modern UI.
- vs. **ContaPyme / Aliaddo**: More basic platforms; Siigo wins on scale (1.2M users), integrations, and brand recognition.

**Social Proof:**
- 300,000+ empresas (businesses) trust Siigo
- 1.2 million users across LATAM
- 93% customer satisfaction score across LATAM
- #1 proveedor tecnológico autorizado por la DIAN en Colombia
- Present in 6 countries: Colombia, Ecuador, Uruguay, Peru, Chile, Mexico
- 2,900 employees

## VISUAL SYSTEM

**Primary Font:** Sans-serif — clean, geometric, high readability; based on the Brandbook IC 2022 reference, likely a custom-licensed or corporate geometric sans (similar to Montserrat or Inter in weight distribution). Headings are set bold/heavy weight; body is regular weight.  
**Secondary Font:** Same family in lighter/regular weight for body copy and UI labels.  
**Letter-spacing:** Tight to normal on headings; standard on body.  
**Distinctive treatments:** All-caps CTA buttons; bold hero headlines with exclamation marks.

**Primary Color:** Blue — corporate anchor color. Based on Siigo's website CTA buttons, navigation elements, and logo, the primary blue is approximately **#005FCC** (medium corporate blue — confirmed blue "Logo Azul" from Nosotros page).  
**Secondary Color:** Lighter blue tint **#E8F2FF** — used for section backgrounds, card fills, highlight containers.  
**Accent Color:** Green **#00C781** (approx.) — used for success states, checkmarks, plan highlights, and positive metric callouts.  
**Background Colors:** White (#FFFFFF) primary; very light blue-grey (#F5F8FF) for alternating sections; dark navy (#0A1628) for footer and dark-mode hero panels.  
**CTA Color and Style:** Primary CTA = solid blue button (#005FCC) with white text, pill-rounded or softly rounded corners, bold all-caps or sentence-case text. Secondary CTA = ghost/outline button in blue or white-on-dark. CTA language is urgent: "¡Comprar ahora!", "¡Empieza hoy!", "¡Quiero cotizar!".

## PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION

**Lighting:** Bright, natural, studio-clean. High-key lighting on subjects. Overcast/soft fill; avoids harsh shadows. Professional but not sterile.  
**Color Grading:** Warm-neutral with slight cool tones. Clean whites. Skin tones are accurate and warm. Minimal Instagram-style filtering — looks editorial, not lifestyle-filtered.  
**Composition:** Person-led compositions — entrepreneurs at laptops, accountants at desks, small business owners with phones. Mix of medium shots (waist-up) and close-ups (hands on keyboard, phone screens). Rule-of-thirds; subject left or right with copy space.  
**Subject Matter:** Diverse LATAM professionals — entrepreneurs (25–45), accountants, small retail/restaurant owners, medical practitioners. Modern office environments, home office, and light commercial spaces.  
**Props and Surfaces:** Laptops, smartphones, tablets showing Siigo UI. Clean desks with minimal props. Occasional coffee mugs, notepads. Light wood and white surfaces.  
**Mood:** Empowered, calm-but-confident. The "I finally figured this out" feeling. Not stressed. Not corporate-stiff. Friendly competence.

## DIGITAL PRODUCT DETAILS

**Key Screens:**
- **Electronic Invoice Creation**: Clean form UI — client selector, product/service line items, tax auto-fill, "Generar factura" button. White background with blue accents.
- **Sales Dashboard**: Real-time summary cards (total ventas, documentos emitidos, por cobrar). Bar charts in blue/green palette. Top nav with Siigo logo; sidebar for module navigation.
- **Document Status View**: List view of invoices with DIAN status indicators (Aceptada ✓, En proceso, Rechazada). Color-coded status pills (green/yellow/red).
- **Mobile App (Siigo App)**: Simplified invoice creation flow. Large tap targets, bottom navigation, card-based layout. Shows dashboard summary with total today's sales.

**UI Style:**
- **Mode**: Light mode primary. White and light-grey backgrounds dominate.
- **Corners**: Softly rounded (8–12px radius on cards and buttons). Not fully pill-shaped but friendly.
- **Shadows**: Subtle card shadows (soft box-shadow, low blur). Elevation is modest — not Material Design dramatic.
- **Illustration Style**: Clean flat/semi-flat icons (FontAwesome Pro + custom icons). No heavy illustration; UI screenshots and isometric device mockups appear in marketing.
- **Data Visualization**: Simple bar charts and summary metric cards. Blue is the primary chart color; green for positive indicators.

**Device Priority:** Desktop-first for accounting/ERP depth. Mobile app secondary for quick invoicing on-the-go. Both experiences are polished.  
**Onboarding Flow Summary:** Registration → DIAN authorization setup → configure company profile → import clients/products → generate first invoice. Assisted by virtual assistant "Claudi."  
**Core User Action:** Generate a DIAN-compliant electronic invoice in under 30 seconds — "Crea facturas personalizadas en 30 segundos."

## SERVICE EXPERIENCE

**Core Problem Solved:** DIAN-compliant electronic invoicing — Colombia's mandatory e-invoicing regulation. Eliminates manual invoice creation, XML structure errors, and DIAN submission complexity. Keeps SMEs compliant without needing accounting expertise.

**Key Outcome (quantifiable):**
- Create and send a DIAN-compliant invoice in 30 seconds
- Reduce invoicing errors to near zero (automatic tax application, CUFE generation, XML validation)
- 93% customer satisfaction across LATAM

**Customer Journey:**
1. **Sign-up**: Register online, choose plan, enter RUT/NIT
2. **Activation**: DIAN resolution sync, digital certificate configuration, company profile setup
3. **Engagement**: Daily invoice generation, client/product import, WhatsApp delivery, real-time sales dashboard
4. **Expansion**: Add payroll (Nómina Electrónica), upgrade to Siigo Nube ERP (full accounting + inventory + POS), add multi-user access

**Integration Ecosystem:**
- DIAN (mandatory government integration — Colombia's tax authority)
- Mercado Pago (payment collection)
- WhatsApp Business (invoice delivery + collection reminders)
- Shopify (via Siigo factory integration)
- REST API for custom integrations
- Siigo Contador (free module for accountants managing multiple clients)

## AD CREATIVE STYLE

**Typical formats:** Static square (1:1) and portrait (4:5) for feed; vertical (9:16) for Stories/Reels. Video testimonials appear but static ads dominate for direct-response. Carousel ads for feature showcases.

**Text overlay style:**
- Large, bold headline in white or dark navy — typically a pain-point hook or benefit statement
- Blue or green accent color for key numbers/highlights ("¡Gratis!", "30 segundos", "300K empresas")
- Sub-headline in regular weight explaining the offer
- CTA badge/pill in bottom-right or bottom-center — solid blue with white text ("¡Empieza hoy!", "Ver planes")
- UI screenshot or device mockup shown prominently — showing the actual invoice creation screen

**Screenshot vs. illustration:** Screenshots and device mockups dominate (builds trust/familiarity). Clean browser-chrome-free screenshots of the dashboard and invoice creation UI, placed on a laptop or phone mockup floating on a gradient background.

**UGC usage:** Minimal. Occasional customer testimonial quotes overlaid on branded color backgrounds. Professional photography (not UGC-style selfies).

**Offer presentation:**
- Lead with the free tier or document volume as the hook: "¡Factúra GRATIS!" or "Desde $9,990/mes"
- Followed by compliance authority: "Autorizado por la DIAN"
- Urgency: "¡Empieza hoy!" — no trial expiry pressure, just activation encouragement
- Trust indicators: "300K empresas confían en Siigo"
